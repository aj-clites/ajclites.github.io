Summary

Andrew is someone who helps people and fixes problems. He's learning about computers and how to help in hospitals. He's really good at making sure things are in the right place,
solving problems, and helping people get what they need. He's had different jobs, like working in a movie theater, a restaurant, and a store. He's also studying and learning special skills
to work with computers and help people with disabilities.

Explanation

Andrew is an individual seeking a career in IT or healthcare, with plans to graduate soon with an associate degree in science. His resume emphasizes his hands-on experience in customer service,
problem-solving, and technical support. He has worked with network infrastructure and tools like PowerShell and Bash for managing files and user permissions, and he's familiar with data analysis
using programs like Google Sheets and Tableau.

In addition to his technical skills, Andrew has developed strong customer service abilities through jobs in retail and hospitality. He's also working towards certications like Google IT Support
Professional Certificate and Six Sigma, which further enhance his qualifications. His education covers a broad range of fields including computer programming, biophysics, and medical lab technology, showing
his commitment to building a versatile skill set.
